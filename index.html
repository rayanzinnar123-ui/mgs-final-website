Characters

Solid Snake

Solid Snake is a legendary soldier and master of stealth, known for his calm demeanor and unwavering determination in the face of impossible odds. Trained for infiltration and close-quarters combat, he consistently confronts nuclear threats and rogue operatives while wrestling with the burdens of his own past. Courageous, resourceful, and morally driven, Solid Snake stands as the embodiment of heroism in the shadowy world of espionage.

Tactical Espionage Expert Marksman Stealth Master

Liquid Snake

Liquid Snake is a cunning and charismatic soldier, driven by ambition, rivalry, and the desire to surpass his “twin,” Solid Snake. A master tactician and fearless combatant, he orchestrates complex schemes and rallies followers to challenge governments and legends alike. Fueled by pride and a deep sense of destiny, Liquid Snake embodies the chaos and obsession that define a true antagonist.

Ruthless Leader Combat Genius Genetic Perfection

Raiden

Raiden is a highly skilled soldier whose journey from rookie operative to cybernetic warrior is defined by struggle, resilience, and self-discovery. Trained in both stealth and close-quarters combat, he faces moral dilemmas and unimaginable threats while uncovering the dark truths behind war and manipulation. Determined, adaptable, and relentless, Raiden evolves into a symbol of human perseverance and transformation.

Versatile Fighter Adaptable Warrior Reluctant Hero

Big Boss

Big Boss is a legendary soldier whose vision and ambition shaped the world of private armies and global conflict. A master of combat, strategy, and leadership, he inspires loyalty while navigating the fine line between heroism and villainy. Complex, determined, and haunted by his past, Big Boss embodies the birth of legends and the human cost behind them.

Master Tactician Living Legend Immortal Soldier

The Boss

The Boss is a legendary soldier and mentor whose ideals and sacrifices shape the course of history. Renowned for her unmatched combat skills and unwavering sense of duty, she navigates the thin line between loyalty and betrayal to protect her nation. Wise, disciplined, and inspiring, The Boss embodies honor and the ultimate cost of serving one’s convictions.

Legendary Fighter Symbol of Honor Tragic Hero

Revolver Ocelot

Revolver Ocelot is one of the most enigmatic and influential figures in the Metal Gear Solid saga. A master gunslinger and elite interrogator, he is known for his obsession with revolvers, his psychological warfare, and his unmatched ability to manipulate allies and enemies alike. Loyal to no one but his own long-term vision, Ocelot operates as a double—and often triple—agent, shaping the fate of the world from the shadows across multiple eras.

Master Manipulator Gunslinger Triple Agent

Meryl Silverburgh

Meryl Silverburgh is a determined and resilient soldier who represents the human cost of warfare in the Metal Gear Solid series. Thrust into combat with limited experience, she proves her courage through grit, adaptability, and an unyielding will to survive. Balancing vulnerability with strength, Meryl stands as a symbol of personal growth and moral resolve amid the chaos of the battlefield.

Rookie Soldier Resilient Strong-Willed

Venom Snake

Venom Snake is the phantom forged from legend, a soldier shaped by trauma, loyalty, and deception. As the commander of Diamond Dogs, he carries the weight of Big Boss’s legacy while carving his own identity through relentless warfare and leadership. Silent, disciplined, and unwavering, Venom Snake embodies the cost of turning a man into a symbol—and the sacrifice required to keep a legend alive.

Phantom Soldier Reluctant Leader Living Legend

Hal "Otacon" Emmerich

Hal “Otacon” Emmerich is a brilliant engineer whose idealism clashes sharply with the brutal reality of war. A specialist in robotics and weapons technology, he provides critical support while struggling with guilt over how his inventions are used. Intelligent, compassionate, and deeply human, Otacon serves as the moral heart of the Metal Gear Solid series, proving that empathy can exist even in a world built on conflict.

Genius Engineer Idealist Moral Compass

Game Glossary

OUTER HEAVEN

+

A legendary military fortress and nation founded by Big Boss. The first game takes place during attempts to stop its weapon.

METAL GEAR

+

A bipedal nuclear-armed tank. The ultimate weapon that becomes the series' most iconic vehicle and symbol.

CODEC

+

A wireless communication device allowing soldiers to receive mission briefings and guidance during operations.

FOXDIE

+

An experimental virus engineered to assassinate specific individuals through genetic targeting.

SHADOW MOSES

+

A top-secret military fortress on an island in Alaska where the first game's infiltration mission takes place.

PATRIOTS

+

The mysterious organization controlling global politics, wars, and the destiny of soldiers throughout the series.
